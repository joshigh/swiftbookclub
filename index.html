About

Not a networking event.
A real conversation.

The Swift Business Book Club meets monthly for a focused, in-person discussion with a small group of professionals who are actively working to improve how they think and operate. There are no presentations, no sales agendas, and no casual attendance.

Every session is built around one book. The goal is not to review it. The goal is to apply it.

Who It's For

The quality of the room
is everything.

Participants should come prepared. The small group format only works when everyone in it is willing to engage honestly and bring something real to the conversation.

A good fit

  • Business owners and professionals actively working on their performance
  • Real estate agents who want more than surface-level talk
  • People who will read — or listen to — the book before attending
  • Anyone who values substance over small talk

Not a fit

  • Casual attendees who come unprepared
  • Anyone looking to pitch a product or service
  • People who want to network more than they want to think
